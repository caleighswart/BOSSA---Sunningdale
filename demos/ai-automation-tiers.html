AI Automation Tiers

Not all automations are created equal. Below is a breakdown of what's possible across three levels of complexity — from quick wins that can be live within days, to intelligent systems that replace entire operational roles.

Simple
Quick Wins
Single trigger, single action. Set up once and they run forever with minimal upkeep.
💬
WhatsApp FAQ Bot
Answers hours, parking, menu and dietary questions automatically
📅
Booking Confirmation
Auto-sends confirmation + date, time, address after every reservation
⭐
Review Request Trigger
Sends "Leave us a Google review?" message 24hrs after a visit
🎂
Birthday Greeting
Sends a personalised discount or message on a customer's birthday
📢
Daily Specials Broadcast
Owner types today's special once — agent sends it to the customer list
📋
Staff Shift Briefing
Generates and delivers a pre-shift brief to the staff WhatsApp group
📞
Missed Call Auto-Reply
Fires a WhatsApp message if no one answers the restaurant phone
Medium
Smart Workflows
Multi-step logic and light integrations. The agent makes decisions, not just responses.
🗓️
Full Booking Manager
Takes booking → confirms → sends 2hr reminder → follows up after visit
😤
Complaint Handling Workflow
Detects negative sentiment, escalates to manager, drafts a response
🏅
Loyalty Points Tracker
Tracks customer visits via WhatsApp and rewards every 5th visit automatically
📦
Supplier Order Assistant
Owner messages "order 20kg chicken" — agent sends structured order to supplier
📊
Weekly Performance Report
Pulls review data and booking numbers into a weekly owner summary
🧑‍🍳
Staff Scheduling Assistant
Staff submit availability, agent builds a draft schedule and sends to manager
💌
Re-Engagement Campaign
Sends "we miss you" promo to customers who haven't visited in 30+ days
Advanced
Intelligent Systems
Multi-system pipelines that think, adapt, and act — replacing significant manual roles.
📈
Revenue Optimisation Agent
Monitors covers, weather, and time — fires targeted promos automatically on slow nights
👤
Customer Relationship Agent
Tracks every touchpoint, builds customer profiles, personalises every interaction
🏪
Multi-Branch Operations Hub
Aggregates data across all locations, flags issues, sends weekly owner briefing
🤝
AI Hiring Assistant
Posts jobs, screens applicants via WhatsApp chat, shortlists candidates for review
🧾
POS Integration Agent
Connects to the till system, tracks best sellers and slow movers, sends daily insights
📦
Predictive Inventory Agent
Forecasts stock needs based on bookings and historical sales patterns
🎙️
Voice AI Agent
Answers the restaurant phone, takes bookings verbally, handles FAQs in natural speech
